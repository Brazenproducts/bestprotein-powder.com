About Us

Independent supplement reviews you can actually trust.

Who We Are

BestMagnesiumGlycinate.com is an independent review site dedicated to helping people find the right magnesium supplement for their needs. We're not owned by any supplement company, and we don't accept paid placements or sponsored reviews.

Our team includes health enthusiasts and researchers who got tired of seeing the same generic "top 10" lists that clearly never tested a single product. We actually buy these supplements, use them, and report what we find.

How We Make Money

We participate in the Amazon Associates Program. When you click one of our product links and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we fund our testing and keep the site running.

Important: our affiliate relationship does NOT influence our rankings. We recommend products based on quality, value, and user feedback — not commission rates. If a product is bad, we'll tell you it's bad regardless of what we'd earn from recommending it.

Our Review Process

  • Research: We analyze ingredient labels, certifications, and manufacturing practices
  • User data: We aggregate thousands of verified purchase reviews to identify patterns
  • Expert input: We consult with registered dietitians and supplement industry professionals
  • Testing: We personally try top-ranked products over 30+ day periods
  • Updates: We revisit our rankings quarterly to reflect reformulations and new products
